**Module B: Emissions Calculation Engine**

*Purpose:* Calculate carbon footprint per job/part

*Technical approach:*

Create calculation logic based on:
```
Total CO2e = (Energy Emissions) + (Material Emissions) + (Consumables) + (Overhead Allocation)

Where:
Energy Emissions = (Machine Hours × Machine Power Rating × Grid Emission Factor)
Material Emissions = (Material Weight × Material Emission Factor)
Consumables = (Coolant, compressed air - simplified for demo)
Overhead = (Facility baseline / total production hours)
```

*Emission factor database to build:*
- Taiwan electricity grid: ~0.495 kg CO2e/kWh (2024 average)
- Common materials: Aluminum (8-12 kg CO2e/kg), Steel (1.8-2.5 kg CO2e/kg), Stainless steel (6-7 kg CO2e/kg), Titanium (20-35 kg CO2e/kg)
- Machine power ratings: typical 3-axis CNC mill (15-25 kW), 5-axis machining center (30-50 kW), CNC lathe (10-20 kW)

*For demo:* Hard-code 5-6 common materials and 3-4 typical machine types. Make emission factors editable in admin panel.

**Module C: Dashboard & Reporting**

*Demo screens to build:*

**Screen 1: Overview Dashboard**
- Total CO2e emissions this month/year (big number display)
- Trend chart: monthly emissions over past 6 months
- Top 5 highest-carbon jobs/parts
- Emissions by material type (pie chart)
- Emissions by machine (bar chart)

**Screen 2: Job/Part Search & Detail**
- Search bar for job number or part number
- Results table showing: job ID, part name, quantity, total CO2e, CO2e per unit
- Click into detail view showing emissions breakdown (energy vs material vs overhead)

**Screen 3: Product Carbon Certificate Generator**
- Input: part number or job number
- Output: PDF certificate with:
  - Company letterhead area
  - Part identification
  - Carbon footprint: X.XX kg CO2e per unit
  - Calculation methodology summary
  - Date and certificate ID
- "Download PDF" and "Email to customer" buttons

**Screen 4: Settings/Admin**
- Manage emission factors (editable table)
- Manage machine catalog (name, power rating, type)
- Manage material library
- Set facility overhead baseline
- Company profile settings
