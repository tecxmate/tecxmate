01 · Principles

Clarity, craft, and quiet confidence.

Four values guide every surface we ship — from the marketing site to the onboarding form to any client-facing tool we build. Hold these before reaching for type, color, or components.

01

Apple-clean by default

System fonts, generous whitespace, neutral grays, subtle shadows. The UI gets out of the way so the work speaks.

02

Purple is a tool, not décor

Reserve #8c52ff for brand marks, primary actions, and section statements — never for decoration.

03

Square, not rounded

Cards, thumbnails, and tiles use rounded-none. Save radius for buttons, pills, and form fields.

04

Glow on hover

Interactive cards light up with a colored border and ambient shadow matching their category — not a blunt elevation.

02 · Brand

Wordmark.

The wordmark is set in Instrument Serif Italic — a balanced, editorial display serif — and is always written lowercase: tecxmate. Display at a minimum clear space of 0.5× cap height on all sides.

01 · CASE
Always lowercase. Never capitalize, never title-case the mark.
02 · ANGLE
A gentle forward italic — kept as a single fluid word without kerning tweaks.
03 · WEIGHT
Thin only. Do not bold, stroke, or outline the wordmark under any circumstances.
04 · COLOR
Primary purple on light, white on primary or black. Never alternate colors per letter.
