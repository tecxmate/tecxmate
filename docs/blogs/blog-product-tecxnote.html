The Trade Show Problem

Trade shows are lead generation machines — and lead losing machines. The math is brutal:

  • 20-30 conversations per day — Each lasting 3-15 minutes
  • 70-85 dB ambient noise — Making notes difficult to hear yourself think
  • No reliable WiFi — Exhibition halls are connectivity dead zones
  • End-of-day exhaustion — When you finally sit down to process leads, you're running on fumes

The traditional workflow fails at every step:

  1. Talk to prospect
  2. Exchange business cards
  3. Scribble illegible notes on the back (maybe)
  4. Repeat 25 times
  5. Return to hotel, try to reconstruct conversations from memory
  6. Enter contacts into CRM days later — with most context lost

By the time leads reach your CRM, the information that makes them valuable — what they care about, what you discussed, what you promised — has evaporated.

How TecxNote Works

TecxNote is designed around one principle: capture everything now, process it later.

The Core Loop

  1. Record — One tap starts recording. Phone stays in your pocket or hand. Works completely offline.
  2. Scan — Conversation ends, scan their business card. OCR extracts name, company, title, email, phone automatically.
  3. AI Processes — TecxNote transcribes with speaker diarization, generates summaries, extracts action items — all attributed to each speaker.
  4. Contact Created — Business card data and conversation intelligence merge into a single, rich contact record.

Total time between conversations: less than 15 seconds. Stop, scan, confirm, start next recording. Processing happens in the background — it never blocks you.

Built for Reality

Every design decision addresses the physical reality of exhibition floors:

  • Noisy Environments — Batch processing with Deepgram Nova-3 handles background noise far better than real-time streaming
  • Offline Operation — Full recording and card scanning without internet; automatic upload when you're back online
  • One-Handed Use — Large tap targets, minimal gestures, designed for standing and walking
  • High Volume — Handles 30+ recordings per day with background processing queues
  • Smart Deduplication — Recognizes when you've scanned the same card twice

What You Get

For every conversation, TecxNote creates a contact record containing:

  • Contact Information — Name, company, title, email, phone extracted from business card

  • Action Items — What you promised, what they requested, extracted automatically
  • Discussion Topics — Tagged themes for easy filtering and search
  • Recording — Original audio for reference when you need exact words
