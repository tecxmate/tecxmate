What is TecxNote?

TecxNote is a mobile app designed specifically for sales teams at trade shows, exhibitions, and networking events. The concept is simple: you record a conversation, scan a business card, and TecxNote does the rest — combining voice and card data into a single, enriched contact record with full context on who the person is, what they need, and what you promised.

No typing. No forgotten details. No more lost leads.

The Problem We're Solving

Trade shows are a black hole for leads. A typical sales rep has 20 to 30 rapid-fire conversations per day, each lasting just 3 to 15 minutes. The current workflow looks something like this:

  1. Talk to prospect
  2. Exchange business cards
  3. Scribble notes on the back of the card (or don't)
  4. Repeat 25 more times
  5. Return to hotel room, try to remember who said what
  6. Manually enter contacts into CRM days later — if at all

By the time leads make it into the CRM, the context that makes them valuable — what the prospect actually cares about, what you discussed, what follow-up they expect — is gone. TecxNote closes that gap.

How It Works

TecxNote's core loop is designed for speed and simplicity, built around the reality of a noisy trade show floor:

  • Record — Tap one button to start recording your conversation. The phone stays in your pocket or hand. Works fully offline.
  • Scan — When the conversation ends, scan their business card. The camera opens instantly with a framing guide. OCR extracts name, company, title, email, and phone automatically.
  • AI Processes — TecxNote transcribes the recording with speaker diarization, generates a conversation summary, extracts action items, and identifies key discussion points — all attributed to each speaker.
  • Contact Created — The business card data and conversation intelligence merge into a single, rich contact card. You now have a complete record: who they are, what you talked about, and what to do next.

The entire cycle between conversations takes less than 15 seconds. Stop, scan, confirm, start the next recording. Processing happens in the background — it never blocks you.

Built for the Trade Show Floor

Every design decision in TecxNote is shaped by the physical reality of exhibitions:

  • Noisy environments (70-85 dB) — We use batch speech-to-text processing with Deepgram Nova-3, which handles background noise far better than real-time streaming
  • No reliable WiFi — Full offline recording with automatic upload when you're back on a stable connection
  • One-handed operation — Large tap targets, minimal gestures, designed for standing and walking
  • High volume — Built to handle 30+ recordings per day with background processing queues and smart duplicate detection
  • End-of-day review — A daily digest view lets you review every conversation, assign contacts, star priorities, and export everything before you sleep

Smart AI, Two Tiers

TecxNote uses a hybrid AI architecture that delivers value at every level:

  • Free tier — On-device transcription and summarization using whisper.rn and Apple Foundation Models / Gemini Nano. Five recordings per month at zero cost.
  • Paid tier — Cloud-powered pipeline with Deepgram Nova-3 for accurate transcription with speaker labels, and Claude Sonnet for rich, speaker-attributed summaries and action items.

The quality difference between tiers is the natural upgrade trigger — free gets the job done, paid makes it effortless.

Who Is TecxNote For?

TecxNote is built for two key personas:

  • Sales Team Managers — You manage 4 to 6 reps at events, take 15 to 20 meetings yourself, and need to debrief the team and consolidate leads the same evening. TecxNote gives you a complete picture without relying on anyone's memory.
  • Sales Reps — You work the booth or walk the floor, having 20 to 30 brief conversations daily. TecxNote captures everything so you can focus on the conversation, not on note-taking.
