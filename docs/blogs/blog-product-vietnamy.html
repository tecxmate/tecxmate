Vietnamese is one of the world's most fascinating languages — rich in tones, elegant in structure, and spoken by over 85 million people. Yet until now, there hasn't been a single app truly purpose-built for learning Vietnamese. Popular language apps offer surface-level content that fails to address Vietnamese-specific challenges. Vietnamy changes that.

The Problem

If you've tried learning Vietnamese with existing apps, you know the frustration:

  • Tone training is an afterthought — Vietnamese has 6 tones that completely change word meaning, yet most apps barely address them
  • Grammar explanations are shallow — Vietnamese grammar differs fundamentally from English; generic approaches don't work
  • No cultural context — Language and culture are inseparable; learning words without context is learning half the language
  • Limited vocabulary — Most apps offer a few hundred words, barely enough for basic tourism
  • No progression path — Random lessons without a structured curriculum from beginner to advanced

We built Vietnamy because Vietnamese learners deserve better.

What Makes Vietnamy Different

Built From the Ground Up for Vietnamese

Vietnamy isn't a generic language app with Vietnamese content bolted on. Every feature is designed specifically for the challenges of learning Vietnamese:

  • Tone Training System — Practice all 6 Vietnamese tones with visual feedback and audio comparison
  • IPA Transcription — Every word includes International Phonetic Alphabet notation for precise pronunciation
  • Northern/Southern Variants — Learn both major dialect variations where they differ
  • Character-by-Character Breakdown — Understand how Vietnamese words are constructed

Scientifically-Backed Learning

We don't guess at pedagogy. Vietnamy implements proven learning science:

  • Spaced Repetition (SM-2) — The same algorithm used by medical students to memorize thousands of facts, optimized for Vietnamese vocabulary
  • Active Recall — Testing yourself is more effective than passive review; our drills are designed around this principle
  • Contextual Learning — Words are taught in sentences, sentences in conversations, conversations in cultural context
  • Progressive Difficulty — Structured curriculum that builds on previous knowledge

Gamification That Works

Learning should be engaging, not boring. Vietnamy keeps you motivated with:

  • Daily Streaks — Build the habit of daily practice
  • Coins and Hearts — Earn rewards for correct answers, lose hearts for mistakes
  • Level Progression — Clear milestones from absolute beginner to conversational fluency
  • Achievement System — Unlock badges for consistency and mastery
