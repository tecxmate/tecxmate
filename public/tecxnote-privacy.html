Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

TECXNOTE ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Audio Recordings: TECXNOTE records audio conversations at your request. Audio files are stored locally on your device by default and are only uploaded for transcription processing when you choose to transcribe them.

Transcripts: When you transcribe a recording, the audio is sent to our speech-to-text provider (Deepgram) for processing. The resulting transcript is stored in your account.

Contact Information: If you use the business card scanning feature or manually add contacts, this information is stored in your account and synced to the cloud.

Account Data: We collect your email address and authentication information when you create an account.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide transcription and AI summary services
  • To sync your data across devices
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To communicate with you about your account

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides enterprise-grade security including encryption at rest and in transit. Audio files remain on your device unless you choose to upload them for transcription.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Deepgram: For speech-to-text transcription
  • Supabase: For secure data storage and authentication
  • AI Services: For generating summaries and action items

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your recordings, transcripts, and contacts at any time. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

TECXNOTE is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
